About

History today has become one of the boring subject which actually it isnt due to the teaching methods and a whole of text which just sits in the books gazing at the student while he is coping in learning it, Our model here comes up with a unique solution of making history be presentable in a gamified form making it not interesting but also interactive at the same promoting learning through playing

Working/How to Play

This is a multiplayer game platform where you can team up with you friends or play with your parents where in the game board is in the form of snakes ladder concept in such a way that the the events determine what would be the nature of it like out of some tiles we have dates or other events encountring them while playing the games could make you go up or come down

Steps to game play

  • Choose Game Board
  • Start the Game roll the dice
  • Look for quick reads when see any
  • Take up quizes to unlock new boards
